Our Story

A Tradition of Excellence

For over 10+ years, our family has been dedicated to the craft of dominican baking. What started as a small home bakery has grown into a beloved destination for those who appreciate the art of traditional baking.

We believe in slow fermentation, quality ingredients, and the magic that happens when we all get together to bake and make wonder for other's to enjoy. Every product that leaves our ovens carries the warmth of our passion.
